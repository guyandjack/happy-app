Technical SEO

Technical SEO consists of optimizing your site's infrastructure to facilitate crawling and indexing by search engines. I work on technical elements that directly influence your ranking : loading speed, URL structure, schema.org markup, XML sitemap, robots.txt file, etc.

I identify and fix common technical errors such as 404 errors, misconfigured redirects, or canonicalization issues. I also optimize your site for irreproachable mobile compatibility and good results on Google's Core Web Vitals.

FAQ

  • How much do SEO optimization services cost ?

    Pricing depends on the scope of the project, the level of competition in your industry, and your visibility goals.
    For a brochure website with 1 to 6 pages, a basic SEO audit starts from CHF 500. This audit typically includes a technical analysis (performance, structure, indexation), on-page optimization (tags, content, keywords), as well as concrete improvement recommendations.
    The cost may vary depending on the number of pages to audit, the depth of the analysis (local SEO, competitor analysis, link building, content strategy), and the technical complexity of the website.
    A comprehensive SEO optimization service generally ranges between CHF 800 and CHF 2’500, depending on the intensity of the required actions (ongoing optimization, content creation, backlink strategy, performance monitoring).
    A custom quote is always provided after analyzing your needs in order to define a tailored and cost-effective long-term strategy.

  • How long does it take to see SEO results ?

    SEO is a medium- to long-term investment, and timelines vary significantly depending on the level of competition and the targeted geographic area.
    In local SEO (for example, for a query such as “web agency in Crans-Montana”), the first results can appear relatively quickly after the website is indexed, generally within 2 to 4 weeks when competition is moderate.
    To secure a stable top 3 position and generate consistent traffic, it usually takes 1 to 2 months of continuous work (page optimization, relevant local content, technical improvements, and Google Business Profile optimization).
    However, for more competitive queries (cantonal, national level, or highly competitive industries), it typically takes 6 to 12 months to achieve significant and lasting results.
    Each SEO strategy is therefore adapted to the targeted market, with progressive and measurable goals to ensure strong long-term visibility.

  • Do you guarantee first position on Google ?

    No, and you should be wary of any such promise. No professional can guarantee a precise position, as Google's algorithms constantly evolve. However, I commit to applying best practices and improving your visibility progressively in an ethical manner (white hat SEO).

  • What are your SEO methods ?

    I use only ethical SEO techniques, compliant with Google's recommendations. My method includes technical analysis of your site, content optimization, creating relevant backlinks, and regular performance monitoring. I constantly adapt to algorithm evolution.

  • Does SEO work for all types of businesses ?

    Yes, but the strategy must be adapted to your business sector, your target audience and your objectives. Whether you're a local SME, a national company or an e-commerce, SEO can strengthen your visibility. I guide you toward the most relevant actions according to your situation.

  • How do you measure the success of an SEO campaign ?

    Success is measured using several key performance indicators (KPIs): improvements in keyword rankings, growth in organic traffic, increased conversion rate, reduced bounce rate, and the overall impact on your revenue. Detailed reports can be provided to track progress and performance over time.

  • Should SEO continue once good positions are achieved ?

    Yes, because SEO is a continuous process. Algorithms change, competitors evolve, and content must stay up to date. Maintaining your positions requires regular optimization: content updates, performance improvements, backlink monitoring, etc.

  • How does SEO integrate with my global marketing strategy ?

    SEO naturally integrates with your digital strategy. It reinforces actions taken on social networks, content marketing, emailing or online advertising. My approach is global and coherent, to maximize your visibility and attract qualified traffic.

  • What happens if I change SEO providers ?

    In case of transition, I conduct a takeover audit to evaluate previous work and identify areas for improvement. I ensure the transition happens smoothly, without data loss or negative impact. All access and reports belong to you, and I provide complete documentation.

  • Does SEO work for new websites ?

    Yes, it's even ideal to think about SEO from site creation. For a new domain, I set up a basic SEO strategy: clean structure, targeted content, correct markup and technical performance. Even if results take a bit more time, starting with good foundations allows you to save time in the long run.

  • How do you stay up to date with Google algorithm changes ?

    I conduct constant monitoring: following Google's official announcements, participating in SEO events, reading specialized publications, and exchanging with the community. This allows me to quickly adapt strategies to search engine evolution.
