Eco-design web

All my sites are developed according to eco-design principles, aiming to reduce their environmental impact while improving their performance:

  • Optimized code to reduce energy consumption: removal of unnecessary code, reduction of requests and lightweight scripts to limit resource usage on client and server side.
  • Compressed images and modern formats (like WebP or AVIF) to reduce page weight, speed up their display and limit data transfer.
  • Green hosting powered by renewable energies and/or certified for its low environmental impact.
  • Fast loading time thanks to a lightweight structure, lazy loading, caching and reduction of external resources — which also improves SEO.
  • Sustainable design designed to evolve over time without complete redesign: modular architecture, facilitated maintenance and compatibility with various browsers.
  • Digital accessibility integrated from design to limit visual overload and improve the experience for all users, including on older devices.
  • Minimalist design favoring the essential: fewer heavy or unnecessary effects, which reduces digital carbon footprint while promoting efficiency.

An eco-designed site is not only better for the environment, but it also offers a better user experience and can help reduce your hosting costs.

SEO Optimization

All my sites are optimized for search engines from their conception:

  • Technical structure optimized for referencing: clear architecture, clean URLs, well-configured sitemap and robots.txt files to facilitate search engine crawling.
  • Optimal loading speed, a key factor for Google: reduction of heavy resources, compression, caching and performance adapted to all networks.
  • Semantic markup and metadataFor better understanding by search engines: correct use of HTML tags (`h1`, `section`, `article`, etc.) to hierarchize information.
  • Responsive design for an optimal experience on all devices: mobile and tablet compatibility, important criterion for Google's mobile-first referencing.
  • Content strategy to improve your visibility: keyword selection, internal linking, regular publication and high-value content.
  • SEO-friendly accessibilityAlternative texts (`alt`), readable contrasts and keyboard navigation, to help both users and search engines understand the content.

A well-optimized site helps you attract more qualified visitors and convert more customers.

FAQ

  • How much does website creation cost?

    The cost of creating a website depends on its complexity, the features desired and the design. A simple showcase site starts from CHF 1,500,(6 pages) while an e-commerce site or a custom web application represents a more significant investment. A detailed quote is always offered after discussing your specific needs.

  • How many pages does a website need?

    The number of pages needed depends on the type of site you want. For a basic showcase site, about 5 to 6 pages are sufficient, including the home page, services, about, contact, and legal notices. If you have specific needs like a blog or testimonials, there can be more. Each project is unique, so the number of pages can vary according to your objectives and the features you want.

  • How long does it take to develop a website?

    The timeline depends on the project complexity. On average, a showcase site takes 4 to 6 weeks, an e-commerce site between 8 and 12 weeks, and a more complex custom project can require 3 months or more. A realistic schedule is always provided at the project launch.

  • What types of sites can you create?

    I design all types of websites: showcases, e-commerce, blogs, portfolios, web applications, intranets and custom solutions. Whatever your activity, I develop a site adapted to your needs.

  • Do you offer maintenance services once the site is online?

    Yes, I offer several maintenance packages including: updates, bug fixes and minor improvements. This allows you to stay focused on your business while I ensure the stability of your site.

  • Will the site be optimized for search engine optimization (SEO)?

    Absolutely. All my sites are designed with an SEO-friendly structure: clean and semantic code, fast loading, mobile compatibility and well-configured tags. I also accompany you to optimize your content and strengthen your visibility.

  • Will the site be compatible with mobile phones and tablets?

    Yes, I adopt a mobile-first approach and responsive design, guaranteeing a smooth experience on all devices: smartphones, tablets and computers.

  • Will I be able to update the content myself?

    I don't use CMS (like WordPress), as these tools are often heavy, energy-consuming and poorly adapted to the eco-design of a showcase site or small size. Instead, I develop optimized custom sites, lighter, faster and environmentally friendly. If you need to regularly publish content (articles, news, etc.), I can create a custom dashboard that will allow you to easily manage these elements, without having to modify the rest of the site.

  • How is site performance measured once it's online?

    I can integrate analytics tools (like Google Analytics) to track key indicators: traffic, user behavior, conversion rate, etc. I also offer regular reports and improvement recommendations.

  • What if I need new features later?

    My sites are designed to be scalable. You can contact me at any time to add features. I will provide you with a clear quote and ensure that the additions integrate perfectly with your existing site.
