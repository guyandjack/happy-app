My ethical and eco‑responsible charter

As a web and mobile developer, I commit to designing websites and applications that respect both users, the environment and good digital practices. I ensure that every aspect of my work complies with the Green IT recommendations. In addition to these eco-responsibility principles, here are the ethical attitudes that guide my approach:

Accessibility for all
Design inclusive interfaces, accessible to people with disabilities, regardless of their device or connection.
Digital sobriety
Prioritize lightweight and optimized code, limit unnecessary resources and reduce the carbon footprint of digital projects.
Privacy respect
Protect users' personal data, by avoiding any excessive collection and respecting regulations (GDPR, nLPD).
Transparency and ethics
Work with honesty, clearly explain technical choices and propose appropriate solutions, without overselling.
Sustainability
Develop scalable projects, easy to maintain, designed to last over time and limit unnecessary redesigns.
Continuous monitoring
Stay informed of the latest technological advances to propose solutions that are both performant, responsible and innovative.
Because digital can (and must) be more respectful,
each project is for me an opportunity to contribute

My work process

I believe that a structured work process is essential for project success. Here's how I collaborate with my clients:

1

Analysis of your needs

I start by understanding in depth your business, your objectives and your expectations. This phase involves detailed discussions to clearly identify your needs and the challenges to be met.

2

Design and prototyping

I create mockups and prototypes to visualize the project. We collaborate closely during this phase to refine the design and ensure it perfectly meets your expectations before moving on to development.

3

Development

I code and develop the features of your site or application using the most suitable technologies for your project. I maintain regular communication to keep you informed of progress and gather your feedback.

4

Testing and optimization

I rigorously test each feature to guarantee a flawless final product. I also optimize performance for a smooth and fast user experience.

5

Launch and follow-up

After launch, I remain by your side to ensure continuous support, resolve any potential issues and implement continuous improvements based on user feedback. A regular monitoring of SEO performance is also ensured to optimize the visibility of your site over time.
